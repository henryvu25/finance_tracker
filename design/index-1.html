My Styles

This style guide was designed specifically for The MoneyJournal finance app. Everything should look sleek and professional, colors should be cool to neutral.

Font

My application utilizes the 'Trebuchet MS', 'Lucida Sans Unicode', 'Lucida Grande', 'Lucida Sans', Arial, sans-serif font-family with black text. This is for easy reading yet straying away from other overused fonts.
